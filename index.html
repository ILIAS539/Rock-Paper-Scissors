How to Play Rock Paper Scissors
Game Rules:
  • Rock crushes Scissors
  • Paper covers Rock
  • Scissors cuts Paper

If both players choose the same item, it's a tie.

How to Play:
  1. Click on Rock, Paper, or Scissors to make your choice
  2. The computer will randomly choose its move
  3. The winner gets a point
  4. First to 10 points wins the game!
PWA Features:

This is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that can be installed on your device. Once installed, it will open like a native app without a browser interface.
